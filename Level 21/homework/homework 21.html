Kiryu

Kazuma Kiryu, also known as Joryu, and Taichi Suzuki, is the former primary protagonist of the Like a Dragon series. He is an agent of the Daidoji Faction, the former fourth chairman of the Tojo Clan, the former manager of Morning Glory Orphanage, and a former taxi driver for Nagasu Taxi.

Majima

Goro Majima is a recurring major character in the Like a Dragon series introduced in the original Yakuza. He is featured as a protagonist in Yakuza 0, Yakuza: Dead Souls, and the Majima Saga of Yakuza Kiwami 2. He is also featured as a supporting character in Yakuza/Yakuza Kiwami, Yakuza 2/Yakuza Kiwami 2, Yakuza 3, Yakuza 4, Yakuza 5, Yakuza 6, Yakuza: Like a Dragon, Like a Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name, and Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth.

Tatsuya

Tatsuya Ukyo is a major character in the Like a Dragon series. He is the protagonist of the spinoffs Kurohyou: Ryu Ga Gotoku Shinsho and Kurohyou 2: Ryu Ga Gotoku Ashura-hen. Formerly a rowdy youth, he becomes involved with the Tojo Clan as a top fighter in their underground coliseum, Dragon Heat, earning him the ring name, "The Mad Dragon of Kamuro".
